About iDEAL Smart Solution
iDEAL Smart Solution is a Nigerian technology company building innovative, affordable, and scalable digital solutions for the education sector.
We started with a powerful Computer-Based Testing (CBT) platform that eliminates manual grading, reduces exam costs, and saves teachers hundreds of hours. Today, our solutions are trusted and actively used by 6+ secondary schools with over 400+ users (students + staff).
Our mission:
To empower every African school with world-class digital tools — built by Africans, for Africa.

Features

AI-Powered CBT Platform (zero manual grading, anti-cheating)
Smart School Management Portal (attendance, fees, results, parent communication)
Custom EdTech & Enterprise Software Development
Multi-tenant cloud architecture (secure, scalable, reliable)
